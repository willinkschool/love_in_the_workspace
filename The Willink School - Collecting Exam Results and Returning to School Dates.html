Collecting Exam Results and Returning to School Dates

 

GCSE Results will be available from 9.00-11.00 am on Thursday 25th August from the Main Hall.

Enrolment on Year 12 courses is from 11.00am  - 12.00pm on Thursday 1st September. Students should bring in their summer work to complete the enrolment process.

Term starts on Monday 5th September for Years 7 and 12, as well as our Year 10 Peer Mentors.

Term starts on Tuesday 6th September for Years 8, 9, 10, 11 and 13.

 

If you are unable to collect your results, please contact Mrs Vause at jvause@willink.w-berks.sch.uk
